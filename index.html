Non-Disclosure & Non-Compete Agreement

AdvancedMarketing.co

Company Information

Sales Representative Information

1. Purpose

The Representative acknowledges that during their engagement with AdvancedMarketing.co, they will have access to proprietary information including but not limited to: client lists, pricing structures, sales strategies, marketing methodologies, internal processes, trade secrets, business plans, and all other confidential business information. This Agreement is designed to protect the Company's legitimate business interests.

2. Non-Disclosure Obligations

The Representative agrees to the following non-disclosure obligations:

  1. The Representative shall not disclose, share, publish, or otherwise make available any Confidential Information of the Company to any third party, whether directly or indirectly, without prior written consent from the Company.
  2. Confidential Information includes, but is not limited to: client/prospect lists, contact information, lead databases, pricing models, proposal templates, sales scripts, cold calling lists, outreach sequences, website development processes, internal tools, revenue figures, compensation structures, and any proprietary business methodologies.
  3. The Representative shall not use any Confidential Information for personal gain or for the benefit of any competing entity.
  4. All documents, files, records, and materials (physical or digital) containing Confidential Information remain the exclusive property of the Company and must be returned or destroyed upon termination of the engagement.
  5. These non-disclosure obligations shall survive the termination of this Agreement and remain in effect indefinitely.

3. Non-Compete Restrictions

The Representative agrees to the following non-compete restrictions for a period of THREE (3) YEARS from the date of termination of their engagement with the Company, regardless of the reason for termination:

  1. The Representative shall NOT build, design, develop, or participate in the creation of websites for any competing website development agency, web design firm, digital marketing agency, or any entity that offers website development services.
  2. The Representative shall NOT engage in cold calling, cold emailing, direct outreach, lead generation, or any form of solicitation on behalf of any competing website development agency or digital marketing firm.
  3. The Representative shall NOT solicit, contact, approach, or attempt to recruit any current or former clients, prospects, or leads of AdvancedMarketing.co for the purpose of offering competing website development or digital marketing services.
  4. The Representative shall NOT solicit, recruit, or attempt to hire any current or former employees, contractors, or team members of AdvancedMarketing.co to work for a competing entity.
  5. The Representative shall NOT directly or indirectly own, manage, operate, consult for, be employed by, or participate in any business that competes with the Company's website development and digital marketing services.
  6. The Representative shall NOT use any sales techniques, scripts, processes, or methodologies learned at AdvancedMarketing.co for the benefit of any competing business.

4. Non-Compete Period

The non-compete restrictions set forth in Section 3 shall be in effect for a period of THREE (3) YEARS, commencing on the last day of the Representative's engagement with the Company, whether terminated voluntarily, involuntarily, or by mutual agreement.

5. Scope & Geographic Limitation

This Agreement applies to any and all geographic regions, territories, and markets in which AdvancedMarketing.co operates or has conducted business, including but not limited to all online/digital markets. The Representative acknowledges that the Company operates in a digital marketplace without geographic boundaries, and therefore the restrictions herein apply globally.

6. Remedies & Enforcement

The Representative acknowledges that any breach of this Agreement will cause irreparable harm to the Company that cannot be adequately compensated by monetary damages alone. In the event of a breach or threatened breach:

  1. The Company shall be entitled to seek immediate injunctive relief, specific performance, and any other equitable remedies available under law, without the need to post a bond.
  2. The Company shall be entitled to recover all actual damages, lost profits, and consequential damages resulting from the breach.
  3. The Representative shall be liable for all attorneys' fees, court costs, and legal expenses incurred by the Company in enforcing this Agreement.
  4. A liquidated damages amount of $50,000 (Fifty Thousand Dollars) per violation shall be assessed for each proven breach of Sections 2 or 3 of this Agreement, in addition to any other damages.
  5. The non-compete period shall be extended by the duration of any breach, so that the Company receives the full benefit of the agreed-upon restricted period.

7. Severability

If any provision of this Agreement is found to be unenforceable or invalid by a court of competent jurisdiction, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect. The court shall have the authority to modify any overly broad provision to make it enforceable to the maximum extent permitted by law.

8. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties concerning the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, negotiations, and discussions, whether oral or written. This Agreement may not be amended or modified except by a written instrument signed by both parties.

9. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the specified state, without regard to its conflict of laws provisions.

10. Acknowledgment

The Representative acknowledges that they have read this Agreement in its entirety, understand all of its terms and conditions, have had the opportunity to seek independent legal counsel, and agree to be bound by its provisions. The Representative further acknowledges that the restrictions contained herein are reasonable and necessary to protect the Company's legitimate business interests.
